VISION

Sustained Excellence in the Engineering Profession

MISSION

To promote the advancement of engineering and to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and ideas. To provide a broad range of services to members, to assist them in developing their careers and to play their full role in contributing to society. To maintain a high standard of the profession, and to raise the standing and visibility of engineers.

GAOLS

  1. Sustainability
  2. Professionalism
  3. Integrity
  4. Excellence
  5. Continuous Improvement
